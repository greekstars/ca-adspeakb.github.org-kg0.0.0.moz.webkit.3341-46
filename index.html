Kamala Devi Harris

Vice President of the United States. U.S. Senator. Attorney General. Advocate for justice, equality, and change.

Learn More

Early Life & Education

Born October 20, 1964 in Oakland, California. A trailblazer from the start.

Family Background

Daughter of Indian and Jamaican immigrants, raised in a multicultural household in Berkeley, CA.

Education

Graduate of Howard University and UC Hastings College of the Law, class of 1989.

Professional Journey

From prosecutor to politician, forging a path of leadership.

District Attorney

Elected as San Francisco DA in 2003, focusing on crime prevention and rehabilitation.

Attorney General

First female, first Black, and first South Asian AG of California (2011–2017).

U.S. Senator

Represented California from 2017 until election as Vice President.

Vice President

Sworn in January 20, 2021, making history as the first female, Black, and South Asian VP.

Key Initiatives & Impact

Championing policies for justice, equity, and opportunity.

Criminal Justice Reform

Advocated for fair sentencing and reducing mass incarceration.

Healthcare Access

Supports expansion of affordable healthcare and protections for patients.

Climate Action

Leads efforts for sustainable energy and environmental justice.
